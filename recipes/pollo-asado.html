Pollo-asado

Description

This pollo asado is super easy and super good.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons white vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 small dried red peppers
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Place onion, olive oil, lime juice, vinegar, cumin, garlic, red peppers, salt, and black pepper in a blender. Blend until smooth. Place chicken in a container or resealable plastic bag and pour marinade on top; toss to coat. Marinate in the refrigerator for 2 to 24 hours.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Grill chicken until no longer pink in the center, 6 to 7 minutes per side, depending on thickness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the centers should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
